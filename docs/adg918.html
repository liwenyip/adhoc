Analog Devices ADG918 - Wideband CMOS 2:1 Mux/SPDT Switch

Bypasses the RF Amplifier during reception to provide a return signal path, and provides a drastic power saving over the relay it replaces.

  • Low Power Consumption (5 µA)
  • Wideband Operation - DC to 2 GHz
  • High Off Isolation (43 dB @ 1 GHZ)
  • Low Insertion Loss (1 dB DC to 900 MHz)
